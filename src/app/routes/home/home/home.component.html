Point of Sales

Version 9.0.0

CUSTOMER REGISTRATION

Used during the auction preview and during the auction by your cashier to register your clients. Please be certain to make use of the Client Registration forms available in the Report application.

AUCTION PROGRAM

Used to record your sales during an auction. Contains information about your art collection.

INVOICING

Check out clients and generate invoices. Your cashier can produce invoices for your clients checking out early. At the conclusion of your auction, both you and your cashier can quickly checkout clients simultaneously.

ELECTRONIC GALLERY

Used to present our premium art collection to your top clientele after your auctions. Contains luxurious works of art that are available directly from Park West Gallery.

SCANNER PROGRAM

Used after each physical inventory to download the scanner information into your POS system. This will help you to keep accurate records of the artwork.
